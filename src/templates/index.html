Features

  • Format Preservation: Maintains fonts, styles, paragraph formatting, and document structure
  • Context-Aware Translation: Ensures consistent translation throughout the document by maintaining context
  • Multiple Languages: Supports translation to/from Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, and more
  • Powered by GenAI: Uses advanced AI for high-quality translations

What's Supported

  • DOCX Files: Full support with comprehensive formatting preservation including bullet points, numbered lists, tables, and paragraph formatting
  • PDF Files: Basic support (text extraction only, formatting may be lost)

Known Limitations

  • Text boxes and shapes: Not translated
  • Table of Contents: Will need to be regenerated in Word after translation
  • Inline formatting: Bold/italic within paragraphs not preserved (uniform formatting per paragraph)
  • Headers/Footers: May not be fully preserved
